Common Scams

  • Phishing & Spoofing: Fake emails or calls claiming to be from banks, govt agencies, or utilities.
  • Subscription Traps: "Free trials" that hide expensive recurring fees in fine print.
  • Emergency Scams: Urgent messages asking for money for a "friend in trouble."
  • Overpayment Scams: Getting a "check" for more than owed and being asked to return the extra.
